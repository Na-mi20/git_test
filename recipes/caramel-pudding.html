Caramel Pudding

In this recipe you need only few ingredients which you have at home

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Essence

Steps

  1. First, you need make the caramel
  2. Pour the caramel into a pot and spread it
  3. Mix egg with some sugar and vanilla essence
  4. Add some boiled milk too
  5. Put the mixture onto the caramel
  6. Put that into stovetop or oven
  7. After 50 minutes the dish is ready
