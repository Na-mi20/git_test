Recipe Title

French Toast

In this recipe you need only few ingredients which you have at home

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Essence
  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. At first mix some milk, sugar and vanilla essence
  2. Dip the bread into the liquid
  3. Spread some butter onto a pan
  4. Fry the bread on both side for few minutes
